THE "WE ARE" ORGANIZATION

Who are we?

WE ARE is a non-profit organization created to meet a need of social change. WE ARE is currently only situated in Philadelphia, PA. It runs through a network of middle schools and high schools within Philadelphia and seeks to empower under-represented groups making them agents for social change.

What is our goal?

WE ARE wants to increase diversity and inclusion in tech and science spheres. WE think that everyone has a voice and anyone can be a catalyst for change if they empowered.

What is our mission?

Our mission is to launch different programmes within high schools and middle schools that emphasize the importance of different people, create opportunities, their viabilities as fabrics of our society, and the importance of their existence/ contribution in the tech/ science realm These programmes include: WACS(We Are Computer Scientists) WAVE(We are Valid in Every way)
